Guess the Number

Game Description:

Welcome to Guess the Number! In this game, your objective is to guess the secret number. The secret number consists of a certain number of digits, and the number of digits will increase as you progress through different levels.

The game rules are as follows:

  • Enter a guess in the input field provided. The guess should be a numeric value.
  • The score will decrease over time, so make your guesses quickly to earn higher scores.
  • To assist you in guessing the number, you can use the 'Get Hint' button. However, using a hint will deduct some points from your score.
  • Ensure that no digit repeats in your guess.
  • Use the 'Admin' button only if you are the game administrator.

Explanation of the Score:

  • For each guess you make, regardless of whether it is correct or not, you earn 10 points. The score increases with each guess.
  • If you use the 'Get Hint' button, it will deduct a certain number of points from your score. The exact deduction may vary depending on the game implementation.
  • Using the 'Admin' button, available only to the game administrator, will not deduct any points from the score.
  • The final score is calculated based on the total points earned throughout the game.
